## DAY 11（木）｜初志貫徹
**フォーマット：1（画像型）／フック：型E（共感フック）**

### 朝投稿（8:00 AM EST）画像あり
```
You had a goal once.

Before the doubt arrived.
Before people told you to be "realistic."

初志貫徹 — Shoshi kantetsu

"Stay true to what you first set out to do."

When things get hard,
the easiest move is to quietly lower the bar.

The samurai called that abandoning your vow.

What did you promise yourself
before fear talked you into a smaller version?

#JapaneseWisdom #Discipline #Bushido
```

### 夜投稿（8:00 PM EST）テキストのみ
```
"Pivoting" is sometimes wisdom.

But sometimes it's just giving up
with better vocabulary.

Know which one it is.
```

## DAY 12（金）｜急がば回れ
**フォーマット：4（ストーリー型）／フック：型C（数字・事実フック）**

### 朝投稿（8:00 AM EST）画像あり
```
500 years ago, Japanese merchants
refused to cross Lake Biwa by boat —
even when they were in a hurry.

They walked around it instead.

急がば回れ — Isogaba maware

"If you're in a hurry, take the long way around."

The boat was faster.
Until the storm came.

The samurai who rushed into battle
without reading the ground
didn't make it to the next one.

Speed without patience is noise.

Where are you rushing
that would actually benefit from slowing down?

#JapaneseWisdom #Strategy #Bushido
```

### 夜投稿（8:00 PM EST）テキストのみ
```
The shortcut cost more time
than the long road would have.

It always does.
```

## DAY 13（土）｜沈黙は金
**フォーマット：1（画像型）／フック：型A（逆説フック）**

### 朝投稿（8:00 AM EST）画像あり
```
The most powerful thing you can say
is sometimes nothing at all.

沈黙は金 — Chinmoku wa kin

"Silence is gold."

You don't have to respond
to everything that challenges you.

The samurai who answered every insult
wasted his sword on empty air.

Silence is not weakness.
It is selection.

What's something you said recently
that would have been more powerful
left unsaid?

#JapaneseWisdom #Stoicism #Bushido
```

### 夜投稿（8:00 PM EST）テキストのみ
```
Before you respond —

ask yourself:
is this adding light,
or just more noise?
```

## DAY 14（日）｜継続は力なり
**フォーマット：2（テキスト型）／フック：型A（逆説フック）**

### 朝投稿（8:00 AM EST）テキストのみ
```
The most talented person you know
is probably not the most successful.

継続は力なり — Keizoku wa chikara nari

"Consistency is strength."

Talent is common.
Motivation comes and goes.

The rarest thing is someone who shows up
on the days they don't feel like it —

especially those days.

The samurai didn't train when inspired.

He trained.
Full stop.
```

### 夜投稿（8:00 PM EST）テキストのみ
```
Every day you don't show up,
someone else does.

That's the only competition that matters.
```

## DAY 16（火）｜千里の道も一歩から
**フォーマット：2（テキスト型）／フック：型A（逆説フック）**

### 朝投稿（8:00 AM EST）テキストのみ
```
The goal isn't too big.

You're just looking at the whole distance
instead of the next step.

千里の道も一歩から — Senri no michi mo ippo kara

"A journey of a thousand miles
begins with a single step."

The samurai didn't calculate the full journey.

He looked at the next step.
Then the one after.

The thousand miles happened anyway.

What's one step you can take
in the next 24 hours?
```

### 夜投稿（8:00 PM EST）テキストのみ
```
The mountain doesn't care
about your plan.

It only counts your steps.

Start walking.
```

Every system — family, company, society —

The nail that gets hammered down
is also the one holding everything together.

Did you hide to avoid it, or stick up anyway?

Drop your answer. ↓

#JapaneseWisdom #Courage #Bushido

What do you practice
when no one's watching
and nothing's on the line?

好きこそものの上手なれ — Suki koso mono no jōzu nare

"What you love, you'll master."

The sword master didn't choose the sword
because it was practical.

Something in him recognized it.

He improved without noticing.
Went deeper than duty alone
would have ever taken him.

That thing already found you.

What is it?

#JapaneseWisdom #Mastery #Bushido

## DAY 19（金）｜艱難汝を玉にす
**フォーマット：4（ストーリー型）／フック：型F（衝撃事実フック）**

### 朝投稿（8:00 AM EST）画像あり
```
Japanese sword makers heat the steel
to over 1,400 degrees.

Then they fold it.
Over 10,000 times.

Not to punish the metal.
To make it unbreakable.

艱難汝を玉にす — Kannan nanji o tama ni su

"Hardship polishes you into a gem."

Raw stone becomes precious
through friction. Pressure. Time.

Not through comfort.
Not through perfect conditions.

The version of you on the other side of this
is the one you've been trying to become.

What difficulty right now
might actually be refining you?

#JapaneseWisdom #Resilience #Bushido
```

### 夜投稿（8:00 PM EST）テキストのみ
```
The fire doesn't destroy the sword.

It makes it.

What's the fire in your life right now?
```

## DAY 20（土）｜負けるが勝ち
**フォーマット：1（画像型）／フック：型A（逆説フック）**

### 朝投稿（8:00 AM EST）画像あり
```
The smartest move on the board
is sometimes to step back.

負けるが勝ち — Makeru ga kachi

"Losing can be winning."

The samurai who stepped back
wasn't retreating.

He was reading.
Watching the opponent exhaust himself.
Waiting for the moment that mattered.

Not every battle is worth winning.

Is there a fight you're in right now
that isn't worth winning?

#JapaneseWisdom #Strategy #Bushido
```

### 夜投稿（8:00 PM EST）テキストのみ
```
Winning the argument.
Losing the relationship.

The samurai knew the difference.

Do you?
```
